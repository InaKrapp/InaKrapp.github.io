## About me

I am Ina Krapp, currently a Ph.D. student in Economics. My primary interest lies in the intersection of Human Geography and Economics, and the contributions Data Science can bring to these fields. I also do creative writing.

## About my projects

I program primarily in R and, increasingly, Python. I am an open source enthusiast, so I plan to publish some of projects on [Github](https://github.com/InaKrapp).
